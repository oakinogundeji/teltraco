About

Since the inception of the company in 2011, the foundation was laid for what quickly became one of the most successful agricultural wholesale companies in Nigeria. That foundation was based on uncompromising integrity and professionalism with our customers and suppliers alike. Throughout the last decade Telios expanded rapidly by focusing on buying and selling Nigerian agricultural products. In early 2010, we established the operating division Telios Commodities expanding into the specialty food commodity markets.
